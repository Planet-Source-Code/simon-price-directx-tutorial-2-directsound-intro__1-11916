DIRECTX TUTORIAL #2 - DIRECT SOUND INTRODUCTION

WHAT'S THIS?

This tutorial is for the absolute newbie to DirectX/DirectSound. In very few lines of code, but with lots of comments, it explains how to load, play and stop a sound file. The sound file can be played once or looped continuously.
